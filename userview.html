Services We Offer

This are the things tha we can offer

Surgical

Our state-of-the-art surgical suite provides for the performance of a wide variety of surgical procedures such as:
fracture repair, exploratory procedures, and routine surgeries such as spays and neuters.

Anesthesia

We utilize the safest available anesthetics to provide an extra margin of safety, especially for our older or high-risk patients. Using the most modern equipment including:
pulse oximetry, electrocardiogram, and blood pressure; the patient's vital signs are monitored during all anesthetic procedures.

Radiology Services

Our on-site, digital X-ray equipment provides high quality radiographs to aid in the quick diagnosis of many disorders.
In addition, this allows same day consultation with board certified radiologists.
